Planet Reading ka Logic

Log puchte hain, planets se market ka kya lena dena? Asal mein, Sun aur Moon ki gravity aur unka magnetic field human psychology aur emotions ko control karta hai. Kyunki market "Fear and Greed" (dar aur lalach) se chalta hai, planets ki position se hum pehle hi jaan lete hain ki mass psychology kidhar mude-gi.
